Smoked Pork Loin

This is a quick and easy pork loin recipe that will not disappoint!


My wife and I have many different recipes for pork loin but this one is the easiest. I will add the others later! This recipe only takes few hours from start to finish and you can add it to many other sides.

Let's Get Started!

Ingredients

  • Get yourself a pork loin!
  • One pack of bacon
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 shallot
  • Swiss cheese
  • Half stick of butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butchers twine

Steps

  1. Put your butter in a pan and melt it.
  2. Dice up garlic and onion and plan into pan in butter and cook down.
  3. Lay out your bacon like lattice so you can wrap the pork loin with it. And lay the pork loin on top of it.
  4. Once your Garlic and shallot is cooked down, pull it out of the butter and evenly spread it over the pork loin.
  5. Evenly add your swiss cheese over the garlic and shallot (doesn't matter if it is shredded or sliced)
  6. Roll your pork loin up and then roll that in your latticed bacon, tie with butchers twine to keep it in a nice roll.
  7. Keep your pork loin out on your counter while you start you smoker at 350 degrees and let is rise to temp!
  8. Once your smoker reaches temp, place pork loin on the smoker and leave it until it reaches 165 internally.
  9. Once it reaches temp pull it off and wrap it in foil and leave it to rest for a half an hour at least.
  10. Last step is to slice and serve! enjoy!
